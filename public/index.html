About the Artist

Ru Ferguson is an emerging artist currently based in California. She has a BFA in Dance Performance and a BA in Creative Computation from SMU. Ru is certified to teach classical Pilates and is currently pursuing a MA in Creative Technology through SMU. She finds much of her inspiration in nature and loves exploring the intersection between dance and technology.
